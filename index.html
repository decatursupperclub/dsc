Sharing Good Food, Building Community

Join us each month for a potluck-style gathering of friends, new and old, from Decatur, GA and the surrounding area. It's free to join, and we welcome people from all walks of life.

Why a Supper Club?

Nothing brings people together like good food. And bringing people together is exactly what we intend to do.

Fighting the loneliness epidemic

At a time when we're simultaneously more connected yet lonelier and more isolated than ever, sharing a meal that you helped prepare with friends and strangers alike is a revolutionary act.

Overcoming obstacles to community-building

Can you believe how expensive everything has gotten?! DSC members host dinner parties at their homes to make it easier to spend quality time without having to spend a lot of money.

Making it easier to meet new people

Whether you're trying to make new friends after graduating from college, moving to a new city, becoming a parent, finding your balance as an empty-nester, or anything else, DSC dinner parties make meeting new friends fun and accessible.

Here's How It Works...

1. Sign up

Fill out the sign up form at the top of this page and and click Subscribe. Check your email inbox and confirm your subscription using the link in the email we'll send you.

2. RSVP to a dinner party

We host dinner parties once a month at one of our member's homes. Keep an eye out for an email in the coming days with the subject line "Decatur Supper Club" for details on our next meetup.

3. Bring a dish and have fun!

We'll send out a dish sign up sheet before the dinner party where people can put down what they're bringing. Got food allergies? No problem! We'll make sure everyone is aware of what food allergies or dietary restrictions to be mindful of for the dinner party in question.
